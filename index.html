Prevention is better than cure

Conducting predictive analysis to highlight the crime zones. Also, Using the Geospatial Data to assist the general public and administrative agencies in better decision making and thus lowering the crime rate.

Roads

Creating crime zone clusters across the Road network in order to identify the hot spots(most impacted areas) and the cold spots (least impacted areas). Also, facilities such as Police Stations, Cellular towers and Hospitals will be mapped across the road network

Highways

It covers the various State and National highway routes. The shortest route covered will encompass State Highways and National Highways. Our priority is to choose the best route among the number of routes available(if any) based on the crime statistics and other factors.

Expressway

It maps the expressways and the various exit points present on it. We are dividing the area regionwise which comes under different administrative regions. The crime analysis reports will ensure that the authority ensures better patrolling in area.

City Roads

It covers the internal road network of a city and list the possible crime zones in the city. The various clusters will be divided based on the crime present in the different area, and the scores will be given based on the analysis. Statistical tools analysis would study the correlation between the crime and other socio-economic factors. It would be helpful for real estate buyers and tenants.

Infrastructure

Educational Institutions

The crimes occuring nearby various educational institutions.

Industries

The crimes occuring nearby to any industries and cases of theft reported (Loss of Manpower and production).

Residential Society

The crimes occuring nearby to any residential society and cases of theft reported.

Shops/Markets

How the Markets/ Shopping areas are impacted by the crime zone. How the shops or markets are affected due to the riots or any theft. Areas which are affected most due to communal tension are identified.

Create Incident Maps

The Public Safety Incident Maps can be used by law enforcement agencies to create a series of incident layers from computer aided dispatch or records management data.

Visualize Crime Patterns

Repeat and Near Repeat Analysis can be used by law enforcement agencies to identify repeat and near repeat victimization patterns of a particular crime type within a specified time frame and geographic area.

Manage Community Events

A collection of maps and apps used by government agencies and other stakeholders to permit, coordinate, plan, and promote community or special events.

Develop Pre-Incident Plans

The Pre-Incident Planning map can be used by fire and law enforcement incident operations personnel to streamline the collection of hazards, equipment, supplies, and procedures needed to deal with a potential incident.

Analyze Cell Phone Data

The Cell Phone Analysis Map can be used by law enforcement agencies to analyze cell tower information and call detail records acquired from wireless service providers.
